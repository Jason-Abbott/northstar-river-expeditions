All rafting and camping equipment is provided, including:

  • Waterproof bags for personal duffel
  • Freshly laundered sleeping bags
  • Inflatable sleeping pads
  • Tents
  • Ground tarps
  • Rain flies
  • Camera boxes
  • Coast Guard approved life jackets
  • First aid supplies
  • Safety gear for paddle teams and kayakers
  • Fishing gear
A hand wash system is available before all meals and in camp. We carry out all human waste in tank toilets, which will also be available for use throughout the day. See our detailed GEAR LIST (online soon) recommending what clothing and personal items you should bring for the trip.
